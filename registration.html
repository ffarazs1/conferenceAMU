Process :
  • Submission Process:

    • The submission and review process for EIT 2021 will be done through EasyChair, an online manuscript management system. The abstracts and manuscripts will be uploaded by the authors using their EasyChair account on or before the deadlines. The authors can also check the status of their manuscript during review process.

  • Review Process:

    • Once the manuscript submission deadline is over, the scientific committee will initiate the review process and further intimate the final outcome to the authors in time. All the manuscripts will pass through a double-blind review process from anonymous reviewers to be managed through the EasyChair publication system. For any sort of problem, that an author may encounter with during review process, the author can contact us.
